INITIATIVES

Sikh Youth Australia organizes various initiatives all year round which bring Sikhs from all around the world together to foster and contribute to the betterment of the wider Australian Community. These initiatives include: Annual Summer Youth Camps, Future Leaders Programs, Mighty Khalsa Fun Days, Global Kirtan Tours & Health/Professional Initiatives. Click below to find out more information on each of our initiatives!

  • Summer Camp

    The SYA Summer Camp is an opportunity to make new friends & have fun, whilst also having the ability to immerse in knowledge and understanding of Sikhism.

  • Global Tours

    These Global Tours are the perfect opportunity to immerse yourself in the knowledge and understanding of Sikhism.

  • Mighty Khalsa Fun Day

    Mighty Khalsa Fun Day is a must go to for all kids to learn about Sikhi & take part it some exciting outdoor activities!

  • Culture Care

    Culture Care is a social healthcare initiative developed by senior youth from Sikh Youth Australia providing healthcare programs to the Punjabi and wider migrant community.

  • Future Leaders Program

    Providing Young Adults with the opportunity to develop their leadership skills, giving them a competitive edge in today’s fast paced world.

  • Young Sikh Professionals Network (YSPN)

    YSPN is a platform created for young Sikhs to build professional connections, succeed, amplify their influence and contribute to the development of the Sikh & broader Australian community.

  • Sikh To Give

    Sikh to Give (S2G) is a charity team established as an initiative of Sikh Youth Australia, to support the underprivileged within the wider Australian community.
Sikh Youth Australia was formed in 1996, and has been a progressive, national organization enriching the lives of Australian Sikh youth and their families by empowering them to understand and enjoy their identity & contribute positively to the social and economic development of Australia.
